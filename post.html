Costa Rica

In the winter of 2019 I was blessed with the opportunity to travel to Costa Rica with my sister and her soccer team. While in Costa Rica we played against three of the local teams and it was so much fun! Having learned Spanish throughout my life I was able to Speak with some of the players and people that we met there.

Not only did we play soccer, we were also able to participate in so many fun activities. We went shopping, to the beach, we visited some little kids and played soccer with them, we went ziplining and saw some beautiful waterfalls. I loved all the experiences that we had and I am so grateful for the memories that I made during my time there.
